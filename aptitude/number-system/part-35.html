Question

A man engaged a servant on the condition that he would pay him ₹ 90 and a turban after service of one year. He served only for nine months and received the turban and an amount of ₹ 65. The price of turban is
  • A: ₹ 25
  • B: ₹ 18.75
  • C: ₹ 10
  • D: ₹ 2.50

Answer: Correct Option: C

Question

If the operation ‘ * ’ is defined by a * b = a + b – ab, then 5 * 7 equals
  • A: 12
  • B: –47
  • C: –23
  • D: 35

Answer: Correct Option: C

Question

  • A: 6
  • B: 3.5
  • C: 8
  • D: None of these

Answer: Correct Option: C

Question
